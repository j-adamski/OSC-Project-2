T3-Todo-List-Time-Tracker

To assist those who struggle with time management through a simple, easy to use, to do list time tracker.

About T3

Time is constantly moving and it takes a lot out of someone to fulfill their goals when they are not running with time BUT against time. It is important to us to live our best quality life and by having T3 we are able to enhance the quality of our life.

As students, we understand perfectly the concept of juggling between task. Thus we decided to create a task management application that will track time spent on assignments and it will have a simple todo list that tracks project time as well as time wasted.
